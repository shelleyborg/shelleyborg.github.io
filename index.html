FACT OF THE DAY

Eurovision has launched the careers of many successful artists, including ABBA, Celine Dion, and Julio Iglesias. It's a major platform for aspiring singers and songwriters.

The Eurovision Song Contest began in 1956 with only 7 participating countries: Switzerland, Germany, France, Belgium, Italy, Luxembourg, and the Netherlands.

The 2020 film "Eurovision Song Contest: The Story of Fire Saga," starring Will Ferrell and Rachel McAdams, brought the contest to a wider audience with its humorous take on the competition.
